About



Oslocutor is a Python3 obfuscator written in... Python3.


It has multiple options allowing you to clean your code, obfuscate all the contents of the variables,

rename imported libraries, rename variables, functions, classes, and even add a shell to your script!


Finally, it compresses your code and adds a final layer, in order to confuse skids even more ;)
